# Product

## Description
Business domain describing Cisco products, offers, product families, technology domains, and business entities.

## Business Purpose
Supports analysis of portfolio mix, offer types, and business entity performance. The business process notes that products are configured in Cisco Commerce Workspace and priced before booking recognition, grounding this domain in the commercial sales flow.
