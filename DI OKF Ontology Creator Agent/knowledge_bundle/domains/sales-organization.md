# Sales Organization

## Description
Business domain describing sales representatives, roles, teams, and market coverage.

## Business Purpose
Supports analysis of bookings by sales representative, role, team, and segment coverage. The business process assigns sales and account executives ownership of the customer relationship and the booking, providing the organizational context for this domain.
