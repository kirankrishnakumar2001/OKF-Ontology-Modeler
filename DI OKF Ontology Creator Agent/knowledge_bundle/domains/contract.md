# Contract

## Description
Business domain describing service, support, or subscription contract context tied to bookings.

## Business Purpose
Supports analysis of term, coverage, renewal behavior, ACV, and TCV relationships. The business process explicitly states that service or subscription contracts are created with coverage level and term after booking recognition, and that renewal obligations are established.
