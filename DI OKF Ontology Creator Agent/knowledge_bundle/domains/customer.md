# Customer

## Description
Business domain describing customer accounts, segmentation, industry, and headquarters geography.

## Business Purpose
Supports analysis of bookings by account, segment, industry, and headquarters geography. In the quote-to-booking process, account executive qualification confirms customer segment and coverage model, making this domain central to commercial context.
